More Information

This course offers an in-depth introduction to Python, covering everything from basic syntax to advanced topics like file handling and modules. You'll gain hands-on experience through practical exercises and projects designed to reinforce your learning.

By the end of this course, you will have a solid foundation in Python programming, enabling you to build robust applications and pursue further studies in data science or software development.
